Company Profile



At Luxzure Trucking Services, our goal is to provide excellent trucking services to our customers. The company was established in 2013, and expanded in 2016, after Kyle Uy, a Cebuano businessman, saw the need for a reliable partner in the trucking industry.

Since then, Luxzure has built its reputation on dependable execution. We take pride in providing satisfaction through our well-regulated operations, and the outstanding cleanliness and efficiency of our equipment and vehicles. We ensure that our team is composed of safety-trained and service-oriented drivers, who operate a well-maintained fleet of trucks, ready to serve local and offshore operations. All our equipment and vehicles are serviced regularly by out team of professionals, ensuring that there is no breakdown or mechanical issues that can affect delivery.
